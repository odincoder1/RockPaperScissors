Coconut Chutney

Description

Indian coconut chutney to be served with dosa and idli (steamed rice and lentil cakes).

Ingredients

  • ½ fresh whole coconut, drained and grated
  • ½ cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 3 fresh red chili peppers, chopped
  • ½ teaspoon mustard seed
  • ¼ teaspoon cumin seeds

Steps

  • 1. Place the coconut and yogurt in a blender or food processor.
  • 2. Process to a paste-like consistency.
  • 3. Transfer to a medium bowl.
  • 4. Heat the oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
  • 5. Stir in chili peppers, mustard seed, and cumin seeds.
  • 6. Cook until mustard seeds start to pop.
  • 7. Spoon over the coconut mixture.
  • 8. Cover, and refrigerate until serving.
